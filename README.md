☢️ HBM’s Nuclear Tech: The Great Port ☢️
“Re-Engineering the Apocalypse for the 1.20.1 Era”
In the golden age of Minecraft 1.7.10, there was a mod that didn't just add items—it added consequence. A mod where progress was measured in megajoules and the cost of failure was a radioactive wasteland. That mod was HBM’s Nuclear Tech (NTM).

We are currently deep in the trenches of a massive, ground-up restoration project. The goal? To bridge the decade-long gap between 1.7.10 and 1.20.1, resurrecting the intricate machinery, the punishing logistics, and the devastating arsenal of the original "Atomic Age" experience within the modern Forge ecosystem.

 Technical Architecture
This isn't a "light" port. We are meticulously translating the chaotic genius of the original source code into a structured, optimized, and visually superior 1.20.1 environment.

The Industrial Hierarchy
The Metallurgy Matrix: From the standard-issue Industrial Grade Copper to the elusive Schrabidium, we’ve registered an expansive periodic table of custom alloys, ingots, and powders.

Logistics & Flow: We’ve brought back the iconic Steam Pipes (Dense and Ultra-Dense) and Universal Fluid Ducts, utilizing modern NBT-tagging for precision fluid filtering.

The Fabrication Chain: Assembly lines are returning with a full suite of Stamps (Wire, Flat, Circuit) and Foundry Molds, ensuring that every plate and bolt is earned through high-tier engineering.

Ordnance & Deterrence: The heavy hitters are back. The Gadget, Little Boy, Fat Man, and the world-shaking Tsar Bomba have been re-coded to bring terror to the modern landscape.

🛠️ Mod Modules (Creative Tabs)
The content is so vast it has been partitioned into specialized tactical divisions:

Ⅰ. NTM Engineering & Infrastructure
The backbone of your facility. Contains the structural steel, the piping networks, the conveyor systems, and the fundamental tools (like the Desh Hand Drill and Geiger Counters) required to survive the industrial grind.

Ⅱ. NTM Resource & Elemental Registry
The raw building blocks of power. This tab houses every state of matter: from raw Beryllium and Thorium to refined Polymer Bars and Reactor-Grade Plutonium. If it can be smelted, crushed, or enriched, it lives here.

Ⅲ. NTM Blueprints & Templates
The "brains" behind the brawn. Includes all Assembly, Chemistry, and Crucible Templates, alongside the newly optimized Universal Fluid Duct system featuring real-time color-coded fluid identification.

Ⅳ. NTM Kinetic Components & Isotopes
The moving parts. Everything from Steel Piston Sets and High-Speed Drillbits to the volatile RTG Pellets and Particle Capsules. This is where the machine comes to life.

⚠️ A Warning to the Engineer
This project honors the "hardcore" philosophy of the original mod. We aren't just porting textures; we are porting the complexity. Expect steep power requirements, dangerous radiation levels, and the constant threat of total facility meltdown.
